Financial Formula (NPV)

Present Net Present Value (NPV):

\( \mathrm{NPV} = \sum_{t=0}^{T} \dfrac{C_t}{(1+r)^t} \)

Where \(C_t\) = cashflow at time \(t\), \(r\) = discount rate.
